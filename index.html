Traditional Learning problems

According to neuroscience research, the essential thing in learning is practice. The more we practice, the more effectively our brain store learned information for later access. However, traditional learning is the opposite of practice. How many times have you opened a code editor and started coding after watching another coding tutorial? Probably not enough. But the problem is not with you. It's the way you learn. Coding videos are designed to be consumed like TV shows. As a result, all the hours spent "learning" turn into hours spent "watching theory." And just theory without practice leads to unproductive learning. Especially in learning to code.
